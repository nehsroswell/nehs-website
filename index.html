Our Mission

Promoting the importance of English and the humanities at Roswell High School, the local NEHS chapter is dedicated to tutoring in english related fields and creating opportunities for Roswell students to get involved in reading and writing enrichment outside of the classroom. We offer writing competitions, workshops, and volunteer opportunities.
